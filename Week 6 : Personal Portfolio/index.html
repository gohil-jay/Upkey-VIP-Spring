About Upkey's vip

Upkey’s Virtual Internship Program (VIP) is a global experiential learning program focusing on an introduction to managerial functions of leadership, finance, marketing, and entrepreneurship.

Upkey’s Virtual Internship (VIP) programs afford young professionals a number of invaluable tools and resources, including deep dives into entrepreneurship, leadership skills, and career readiness from industry experts, access to hands-on projects relevant to your interests and goals, connections with leading companies, entrepreneurs, top professors, executives, and mentors who have been in your shoes before and will support your growth, an ever-expanding network (on average, VIP students triple their network and connections in 8 weeks or less), chances to hop on to other projects across different fields (ex. finance, marketing, and technology) & access to top executives and mentors on leadership training and global initiatives.

The program ran through April 12 2021 till May 21 2021, covering various aspects including leadership, finance, marketing, and entrepreneurship; including weekly presentation and theme oriented projects (of which I passed all) which can be found in upcoming sections.
